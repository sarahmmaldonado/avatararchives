Frequently Asked Questions

What is Avatar: The Last Airbender?
It is a fantasy animated series that follows the journey of Aang, the Avatar, as he works to bring peace to the world.
Who are the main characters?
Aang, Katara, Sokka, Toph, and Zuko are the main characters who form the central team of the series.
How many seasons are there?
There are three seasons: Water, Earth, and Fire.
