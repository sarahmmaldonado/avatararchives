More About Avatar: The Last Airbender

What is Avatar: The Last Airbender?
Avatar: The Last Airbender is a Nickelodeon original TV series that follows the last Airbender, a young boy named Aang, and his three friends around the world. Aang, who is revealed to be the Avatar, is forced to follow his destiny which is to stop the war and save the world. Will Aang and his team of young heroes be able to stop the Fire Nation, or will balance forever be lost?
Why is it so popular?
Its rich storytelling, unique world-building, and well-developed characters make it a beloved show...

What is Aang's role in the series?
